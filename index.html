Building Surveillance

Taiwan

In order to provide a reliable, delightful and positive work environment, we design this system to deploy ultimate surveillance coverage, helping security guards, managers to monitor and protect property of the company.

Discover & Define

Before design this system, we did field study and qualitative research with security guards in company to empathy their needs and pain points. Then we invited stakeholders to join the small workshop to do ideation by using value proposition canvas.

Develop

Those information and resources become nutrients of our design. After several rounds of discussions with each stakeholders and we also considered about the costs, time and technical feasibility. We separated this surveillance platform into three parts: Central Control System (PC view & mobile view), Vendor System, and Permission Setting.

Hospital Hygiene

KIDS | Germany

Hospital Hygiene --- Project of Köln International School of Design
(Germany, leaded by Prof. Birgit Mager --- president of Service Design Network)

Cleaning services are at the lowest range of the service industries – badly paid and not valued. But especially in hospitals cleaning is an important factor for hygiene and closely related to the topic of hospital-infections. This is one of the big challenges of the German health system.
